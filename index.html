About Tom Thomas

Music is special isn’t it? There’s nothing else to the best of my knowledge that can take you to a whole other place emotionally, mentally, and spiritually like the right songs. I was exposed to everything from Bob Dylan to Naughty By Nature at a very young age and being introduced to such a wide variety of artists early on definitely sparked something in me, with my earliest songs tracking back to the age of 4 and earliest demos to the age of 8.

While the songs may not have been the deepest (a lot of them were about Pokemon), they carried a lot of meaning to me. Regardless of what kind of tune I was churning out, it always came together very quickly. There was never a lot of trouble with trying to find the right words to say (I wish this was the case outside of music also but hey).

I didn’t have a lot of extracurriculars like the other kids, I wasn’t into sports (I played Timbits Soccer until I got yelled at too much since I couldn’t figure out what an offside was), my friend group was small, and my mother was quite overprotective of me so this left me with a lot of time on my hands in my younger years. Music and video games took up the majority of my days.

High school seems like a blur to me, except for my last year. I had just gotten out of a long term on-off relationship (which inspired a LOT of music), so I was in a bit of a crisis to figure out my own identity as I wasn’t living as an individual for the most of my teenage years. I thought I needed to rebel and experiment as I was starting to get in with the “cool kids” and mirroring their behavior (which didn’t come naturally to me). However, when we were on our senior trip close to the start of my final year I had a breakthrough.

There was a musician playing for us at the resort who after his set had offered up the stage to anyone willing and I don’t know what it was that pulled me up there (I had never performed in front of anyone at this point) but something inside me needed to go on that stage and in that moment, I found what came naturally to me. This changed everything. My path, my mindset, and my peers.

As I stepped into the real world and the rat race that came along with it, I still made sure to find the time to hone in on this part of my soul and as time went on I found myself making it more and more of a priority in my life. No matter what field or environment I was in (and I’ve been in plenty over the past 6 years) one thing remained the same, while everything else seemed to change. The more effort I put into perfecting my craft and putting my art out there over the years, the more I began to feel like I was coming into my own.

My goal through releasing music is to help as many people as possible get to the place that music got me. Not caring about what the world may think and being the most authentic version of themselves possible in the process. I hope to convey insights into life, love, and longings for the future in these pieces of my soul that I intend to keep sharing with you all.

Music

With each piece of my soul I put out into the universe, there will always be a story behind it. You will be able to find these stories here upon each release.

Seize
3430
75
75

Seize is a track about making the most of each and every day, something I, and many others, have struggled to do, lockdown after lockdown. The song touches on themes of moving forward, getting in touch with our inner power, The song touches on themes of moving forward, getting in touch with our inner power, and being hardened from the past.

The perspective portrayed in this song is that nothing is able to affect us unless we let it and that the past has taken much more of a toll on us than it should have. However, we've learned from our mistakes and are moving on. We now see that the sky's the limit and that we will push past this limit, since we are now living each day like it is our last.

We don't know what the future has in store, but we can give ourselves the security that it will be worth the journey by doing everything we can daily to set ourselves up for success.

This is a theme song for those of us who are constantly striving to get in touch with our best selves, regardless of what anyone else has to say, or what struggles we meet along the way.

Streams

During the pandemic, many of us were lost for how to spend our time and I was no different. I was looking for connections and a way to still showcase my music to the world, this is where livestreaming entered my life. Through something I had originally thought was just for video games, I forged lifelong connections with other artists around the world and was able to find an outlet to share not just my music but my personality on a global scale.

Some of my best memories from the past couple years I have livestreaming to thank for and even though the world is now at a point where live music exists again, my streams will still be an integral part of my journey as a musician! If you are not able to see me perform in person then I cordially invite you to do the next best thing and catch me on Twitch!
