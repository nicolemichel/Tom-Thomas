ABOUT TOM THOMAS

Tom Thomas provides a unique live experience with his raspy tonalities and old school flare. His implementation of live loops allows for a very full sound with tonal elements you wouldn’t expect out of a solo act. Mixed in with his smooth freestyle verses and heavy pop/rap influence, this blend gives everyone something to enjoy and a sound that is easy going but exciting to listen too.

I was exposed to everything from Bob Dylan to Naughty By Nature in my youth & hearing such a variety of artists early on must've sparked something in me, with my first songs being written when I was 4 and earliest demos recorded when I was 8. The songs weren't the deepest (most were about Pokemon), but they carried lots of meaning to me.

High school seems like a blur to me, except for my last year when I realized this was all I ever wanted. I had just gotten out of a long term on-off relationship (which inspired LOTS of music), so I was in a bit of a crisis to figure out my own identity as I wasn’t living as an individual for the most of my teenage years.

We had a musician playing at the resort we stayed at for our senior trip who after his set offered up the stage. I had never performed in front of anyone at this point, but something inside me needed to go on that stage. From then on, everything changed. No matter what field entered in the real world, one thing remained while everything else seemed to shift. The more effort I put into perfecting my craft, the more I felt like I was coming into my own.

My goal through releasing music is to help as many people as possible get to the place that music got me. Not caring about what the world may think and being the most authentic version of themselves possible in the process. I hope to convey insights into life, love, & longings for the future in these pieces of my soul that I intend to keep sharing with you all.

MUSIC

With each piece of my soul I put out into the universe, there will always be a story behind it. You will be able to find these stories here upon each release.

Seize is all about making the most of each day, something I think we all struggled with lockdown after lockdown. The best version of yourself is inside of you and it won’t be tapped into unless you consciously spend your time bettering yourself and not letting outside/past influences hold you back.

You may have let who you were affect who you are now, but you’ve learned from your mistakes and are pushing past the boundaries you’ve set for yourself. This song is all about moving forward, getting in touch with your inner power, and striving to be what you know you were always meant to be.

Only is based on the feeling when you know that you’ve met that special someone and want nothing more than to express these feelings to them. However, it’s deeper than that. You've been out of a relationship for so long that you have no idea how to properly express these emotions to this individual, this song is the embodiment of that expression.

You’ve always told yourself that a relationship isn’t for you and that you’re better off on your own but there’s just something about this person that makes you want to throw that philosophy out the window and give love a go again. This is a theme song for those who are looking to admit their emotions to someone that may just be worth it.

STREAMS

During the pandemic, many of us were lost for how to spend our time and I was no different. I was looking for connections and a way to still showcase my music to the world, this is where livestreaming entered my life. Through something I had originally thought was just for video games, I forged lifelong connections with other artists around the world and was able to find an outlet to share not just my music but my personality on a global scale.

Some of my best memories from the past couple years I have livestreaming to thank for and even though the world is now at a point where live music exists again, my streams will still be an integral part of my journey as a musician! If you are not able to see me perform in person then I cordially invite you to do the next best thing and catch me on Twitch!
